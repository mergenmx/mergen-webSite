Timeline

The path of our development from foundation to future vision.

Foundation October 2025

MergenMX was founded

MergenMX was founded in October 2025 as a team of two. This was the starting point where we clarified our vision and laid the foundation for the products we wanted to build in the future.

Growth December 2025

Our team grew to four

In December 2025, two more people joined us. This gave us a stronger team structure and allowed us to start working actively on our projects.

Launch March 2026

We launched our first two apps

In March 2026, we launched our first two apps. This became one of the most important milestones where our ideas started turning into real products.

Goal Rest of 2026

5 more apps in total

Our goal for the rest of 2026 is to release 5 more apps in total. This will help us expand our product range and create solutions in a wider set of areas.

Vision 2027

Institutional sales and professional software

In 2027, we aim to build more advanced professional software and offer it to institutions. In the long term, we want to become a strong and lasting technology brand.
